Tic Tac Toe

A 2-player Game

How to play

Goal: Get three of your markers in a row, column, or diagonal.

  • Player 1: Click on a cell to place your marker (Player 1: X).
  • Player 2: Click on a cell to place your marker (Player 2: O).
  • The first player to align three markers in a row, column, or diagonal wins.
  • Use the Undo button to revert the last move.
